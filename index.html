Crystals Collector

Instructions

The goal of this game is to increase your score to reach the number below.

Each of the crystal buttons have a different value. Click the buttons to increase your score, and try to reach the number!

If you reach the target number exactly, you win! If your score goes over the target number you will lose the round.

Once you win or lose, the target number will change, and the values of each crystal will change as well.
